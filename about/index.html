Abdallah Hussein Sham

AI Researcher | Educator | Junior Research Fellow

Professional Summary

An Artificial Intelligence researcher with expertise in multimodal emotion recognition, speech technologies, and applied machine learning. Abdallah combines academic rigor with practical application, focusing on how AI can understand human behavior while ensuring fairness, ethics, and bias-awareness in intelligent systems. With a growing body of peer-reviewed publications, he is dedicated to making AI concepts accessible to students and professionals.

Key Experience (Tallinn University & Research Projects)

  • Conducted research on speech emotion recognition using deep neural networks, mapping emotions to Valence-Arousal-Dominance (VAD) space.
  • Developed multimodal AI models integrating voice and behavioral data for improved emotion recognition accuracy.
  • Published and presented in international conferences and journals on AI, ethics, and applied ML.
  • Active collaborator on cross-disciplinary research teams, bridging AI with psychology and human-computer interaction.

Certifications & Academic Achievements

  • Junior Research Fellow – Tallinn University
  • Multiple peer-reviewed AI research publications
  • Recognized contributor in ethical AI and applied machine learning.
